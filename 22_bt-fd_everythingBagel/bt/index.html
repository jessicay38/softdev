Everything Bagels

Bagels with a mix of delicious toppings



Origins

David Gussin claims to have invented it in 1980 by using poppy seeds, sesame seeds, salt, and onion.

Seth Godin claims to have worked in a bagel factory that made everything bagels in 1977.

Brandon Steiner claims to have invented it in 1973 by experimenting with different toppings in a bakery.

The origins of the everything bagel are disputed, but it is agreed that it was invented in the 1970s when bagel makers were experimenting with new flavors.
